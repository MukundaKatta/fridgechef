# FridgeChef

> What's for dinner? Ask your fridge.

Open the door. Snap a photo. Get three dinner ideas using only what's inside. No grocery trip required.

## What you get

- **Sees everything** — Identifies 500+ ingredients from one photo. Even the sad wilted kale in the back.
- **Filters by time** — 15 minutes or an hour? Veggie, meat, pasta? We pick recipes that actually fit.
- **Skips missing items** — No recipe that needs an ingredient you don't have. Genuinely uses what you own.

## Category

Consumer AI. Part of a 50-product exploration of high-demand consumer and SMB markets.
